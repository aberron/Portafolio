Ambulante

2020

The objective of this project was to design the web and social media identity for the Itinerant Documentary Film Tour, Ambulante16. Ambulante16 has a strong bet into the digital media. Curiosity and discovery describe Ambulante’s attitude towards cinema. Under these concepts the proposal was designed. For this project, understanding the information and setting typographical hierarchies was fundamental for its clean structure and easy navigation. This project was a collaboration with Amanda Fernandez, designer and friend.
